# Thomas Laurent, Ph.D

## Expert in biostatistics and data science - Biostatistics training | RWD analysis | Healthcare

[My LinkedIn profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/thomas-laurent-ab161948/)

Thomas is involved in study planning/consultation and statistical analysis, especially real-world database studies (including a variety of Japanese claims databases) and questionnaire validation for pharmaceutical companies. In particular, Thomas Laurent has expertise in various therapeutic areas including oncology, infectiology, cardiovascular diseases and pain. Before joining Clinical Study Support, he had an academic position in Nagoya University (Nagoya, Japan) for two years, focusing mainly on omics data analysis. Moreover, Thomas Laurent has experience in France as a clinical research associate for Cegedim Strategic Data (IQVIA at present) for almost two years, involved mainly in phase I and phase II clinical studies in oncology, and observational prospective studies in various areas. Thomas Laurent holds a Ph.D. in Biomolecular Sciences obtained in Nagoya University (Japan), and a master degree in agricultural engineering at Agrocampus Ouest (Rennes, France). He also holds another master degree in statistics and econometrics from Toulouse University (Toulouse, France). He has participated in various works published (including Real-World research) in Drugs - real world outcomes, BMC musculoskeletal disorders, Evidence-based Complementary and Alternative Medicine: eCAM,  Journal of infection and chemotherapy : official journal of the Japan Society of Chemotherapy, Biology open, Journal of cellular physiology, Scientific reports.
